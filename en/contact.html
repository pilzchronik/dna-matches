Contact, Imprint and Privacy Notice

About me

My name is Wolfgang Pilz. I have been researching my family history for years using classical genealogical methods, and more recently with autosomal DNA analysis. This website documents a part of that work and is addressed to people whom I have contacted as DNA matches, as well as to family members and fellow genealogists.

The project is strictly private. It pursues no commercial or financial interest and is not produced on behalf of any institution.

Privacy Notice

Hosting. This site is served as static content via GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). On each request, technical connection data (in particular IP address, timestamp, requested URL, user agent) is briefly processed by the host. Legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a stable delivery of the site). Details: GitHub Privacy Statement.

No cookies, no tracking. The site sets no cookies, embeds no analytics, advertising or social-media services, and transmits no visitor data to third parties. There is no contact form; communication is possible only through the e-mail address above.

Display of DNA matches. This site summarises results from genealogical DNA platforms (GEDmatch, FamilyTreeDNA, MyHeritage). The names of publicly visible test takers — where they refer to living persons — are shown abbreviated to first name and initial. Platform usernames are kept, as they are not real names. Kit identifiers of other people's tests are not published. Historical persons (meaning already deceased ancestors and named witnesses from publicly documented contexts) are named in full.

Legal basis for this display is the legitimate interest in scientific and genealogical research (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in conjunction with the research privilege (Art. 89 GDPR), balanced against the protected interests of the persons concerned. Data minimisation is achieved through the abbreviation described above.

Your rights. If you are a living person and identifiable on this site, you have the right of access (Art. 15 GDPR), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18) and objection (Art. 21) at any time. A message to the e-mail address above is sufficient; the respective entry will be removed or further anonymised promptly. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (dsb.gv.at).

Retention. Incoming e-mails are kept only as long as necessary for the genealogical correspondence and are deleted thereafter.
